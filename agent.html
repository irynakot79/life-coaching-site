Quiet Guide

A small helper, still being written.

The idea is simple. You say, in a few words, what you are carrying today. It points you to a note, a letter, or an evening that might fit. A small map, nothing more.

What it will help you find

  • a note in the archive, when you don't yet know what you are looking for
  • a quiet way of being together — a letter, a circle, a conversation, a video, an evening
  • a first question, when words are still far away

The Quiet Guide isn't finished yet. Until then, if you want to ask something, a few lines by letter are enough.

Write me

The Quiet Guide is not a therapist, and not a substitute for one. It is only a way of finding your way around the garden.
