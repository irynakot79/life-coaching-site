Written opening

A limit doesn't have to be hard to be real. This short video is about the difference between protecting yourself and closing down — and about how many of us learned to do the second when only the first was asked of us.

And about the other side of the same thing: what happens when a yes slips out of us too quickly — out of habit, or quiet fear — and how to notice that gently, in time.
