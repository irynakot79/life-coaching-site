When duty replaces desire

A short preface on how obligation can become louder than inner direction.

Sometimes we are praised for being reliable when in fact we are quietly disappearing. Duty can look like strength, yet inside it may feel like distance from our own desire.

This reflection invites a slower question: where am I choosing from fear of disappointing others, and where am I choosing from a living yes inside myself?

Public reflections are general and educational. They are not psychotherapy, diagnosis, or crisis care.
